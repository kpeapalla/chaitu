Nara Chandrababu Naidu is an Indian politician hailed for his futuristic outlook.

In a public life spanning for more than four decades, he has to his credit many trail-blazing initiatives that brought about revolutionary changes in the economic and social life of the people. Known for embracing technology to make governance people-friendly, he has been a pioneer in furthering the knowledge economy.
