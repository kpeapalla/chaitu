Telugu Desam Party (TDP)

The Telugu Desam Party (TDP) is a regional political party in India. It was founded by Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (N. T. Rama Rao) on 29 March 1982 with the aim of promoting the welfare, development, and self-respect of the Telugu-speaking people.

The party has played an important role in the politics of Andhra Pradesh. It has formed the state government several times and has focused on infrastructure, education, agriculture, and technology-driven governance.

The current president of the party is N. Chandrababu Naidu. Under his leadership, the party has emphasized digital governance, economic growth, and public welfare initiatives.

Main Objectives

  • Development of Andhra Pradesh
  • Support for farmers and rural development
  • Employment opportunities for youth
  • Improving education and healthcare
  • Infrastructure and technology development
